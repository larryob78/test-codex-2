Blend your camera feed with animated GIFs and text overlays in real-time.

📱 Phone setup tips
  1. Serve this folder from your computer (for example python -m http.server 8000).
  2. Join the same Wi-Fi network on your phone and computer.
  3. On your phone browser, visit the computer’s IP address (e.g. http://192.168.1.42:8000).
  4. Tap Start camera and allow the permission prompt.
  5. Add GIFs or text overlays, then capture stills to download.
